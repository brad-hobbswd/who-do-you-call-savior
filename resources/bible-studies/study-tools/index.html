Equip Yourself

Tools That Help You Grow

Bible study tools are designed to support your time in God's Word, not replace it. Reading plans, study sheets, journals, memory verse cards, and character studies can help you stay organized, understand Scripture more clearly, and remember what God is teaching you.

As you use these resources, remember that the greatest teacher is God's Word itself. Pray before you study, compare Scripture with Scripture, and allow the Holy Spirit to guide your understanding as you continue growing in faith.

Thy word have I hid in mine heart, that I might not sin against thee.
Psalm 119:11

A Prayer Before You Study

Teach Me Through Your Word

Heavenly Father,

Thank You for giving us Your Word. Help me to study it carefully, understand it correctly, and live it faithfully. May every reading plan, worksheet, journal, and study guide lead me back to Scripture and help me know You more deeply.

Give me wisdom as I learn, humility as I grow, and a heart that delights in Your truth. May Your Word become the foundation for every decision I make and every step I take.

In the name of Jesus Christ, Amen.
