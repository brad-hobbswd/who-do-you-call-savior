Biblical Resources

Growing in Jesus Christ does not end when you finish a book. It begins with a daily walk in God's Word. These resources have been created to help you study the Scriptures, strengthen your faith, deepen your prayer life, and continue growing in your relationship with Jesus Christ.

The Bible First

God's Word Is Our Foundation

Every article, Bible study, reading plan, and resource on this website is intended to encourage you to open God's Word for yourself. These studies are not meant to replace your personal time in Scripture but to help guide you through it.

Always test what you read against the Bible. Pray, study, and allow the Holy Spirit to lead you into truth. My prayer is that every resource points you beyond this website and into a deeper relationship with Jesus Christ.

Daily Discipleship

Simple Ways to Grow Every Day

Spiritual growth is not built in a single day. It is the result of faithfully walking with Jesus Christ one day at a time. These simple practices can help you remain rooted in God's Word and continue growing in your relationship with Him.

Read the Bible

Spend time in God's Word every day. Even a single chapter can strengthen your faith and help you know the Lord more deeply.

Pray Daily

Talk with God honestly. Thank Him, worship Him, confess your sins, and bring every concern before Him in prayer.

Memorize Scripture

Hide God's Word in your heart so His truth will guide your decisions and strengthen you during every season of life.

Worship the Lord

Set your heart and mind on Jesus Christ through worship, thanksgiving, and praise each day.

Serve Others

Look for opportunities to encourage, help, and love those around you as Christ has loved you.

Share Your Faith

Tell others about the hope you have found in Jesus Christ and be ready to give an answer with gentleness and respect.
