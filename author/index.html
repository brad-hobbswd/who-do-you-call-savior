About the Author

Bradley Hobbs

Author. Teacher. Follower of Jesus Christ. My greatest desire is that every word I write points people to the Savior who has changed my life.

Welcome

It's a Pleasure to Meet You

Welcome, and thank you for taking the time to visit my website.

My name is Bradley Hobbs, and before I am an author, I am a follower of Jesus Christ. Everything I write is rooted in one desire: to glorify God, remain faithful to His Word, and encourage others to know Him more deeply.

Like every person, my journey has included seasons of joy, seasons of growth, and seasons that tested my faith. Through each chapter of life, I have discovered that God's grace is sufficient, His promises remain true, and His love never fails. Those experiences have shaped both my life and the words that fill these pages.

Writing has become one of the greatest privileges God has given me. Every book begins with prayer, is guided by Scripture, and is written with the hope that someone will come to know Jesus Christ more personally. My desire has never been simply to publish books, but to share the life changing message of the gospel.

If my writing encourages someone to open the Bible, strengthens a weary believer, or points even one person toward Jesus Christ, then I believe the work has been worthwhile.

My Story

A Life Shaped by Grace

God has faithfully led me through many seasons of life, each one teaching lessons that could never have been learned in comfort alone. He has shown me His faithfulness in moments of uncertainty, His strength in times of weakness, and His peace when life seemed overwhelming.

Those experiences have given me a deeper appreciation for the power of God's Word and a greater compassion for people who are searching for hope. They have also reminded me that no one is beyond the reach of God's mercy and that every life can be transformed through Jesus Christ.

That conviction shapes everything I write. Whether the subject is grace, forgiveness, repentance, spiritual growth, or the hope of salvation, my desire is always to lead readers back to the One who changes lives.

My Calling

Serving Through Writing, Teaching, and Ministry

God has opened doors for me to serve through teaching, ministry, and writing. Although each opportunity has been different, they all share the same purpose: to encourage people, strengthen their faith, and faithfully proclaim the truth of God's Word.

Whether I am teaching children, writing a new book, leading a Bible study, or simply encouraging someone one conversation at a time, my prayer is always the same: that Jesus Christ would be seen more clearly than I am.

I believe our world does not need more opinions about Jesus. It needs people who will lovingly and faithfully point others to Him through both their words and their lives.

Writing Ministry

Every Book Begins with Scripture

Every book I write begins in the same place: prayer and the Word of God. My goal is never to write simply to be heard, but to faithfully communicate biblical truth with clarity, conviction, and compassion.

The books on this website explore different subjects, yet they all point to the same unchanging foundation. Whether the topic is salvation, grace, repentance, forgiveness, spiritual authority, or Christian living, every message is centered on Jesus Christ and the transforming power of His gospel.

I believe the Bible is still God's living Word. It continues to change hearts, strengthen believers, and reveal the hope that is found in Christ alone. My prayer is that every reader is encouraged not only to finish a book, but to continue searching the Scriptures long after the final page has been turned.

Beyond the Books

Faith, Family, and Purpose

While writing has become an important part of my ministry, it is only one part of who I am. I am grateful for the opportunities God has given me to serve through teaching, to encourage others in their walk with Christ, and to invest in the lives of those around me.

Family is one of God's greatest blessings in my life. Their love, encouragement, and support have strengthened me through both joyful seasons and difficult ones, reminding me daily of God's faithfulness and His unfailing grace.

Above all, my greatest desire is to live a life that honors Jesus Christ. Everything else, whether writing, teaching, or ministry, flows from that commitment.

A Personal Thank You

My Prayer for Every Reader

Thank you for taking the time to learn a little about my story. It is a privilege to share these books with you, and I never take for granted the opportunity to encourage someone through God's Word.

If there is one thing I hope you remember after leaving this website, it is not my name but the name of Jesus Christ. He is the reason these books were written, the foundation of every message, and the only Savior who gives lasting hope.

May the Lord bless you as you continue your journey with Him. May your faith grow stronger, your love for His Word deepen, and your confidence in His promises increase with each passing day.

Looking unto Jesus the author and finisher of our faith.
Hebrews 12:2
