Meet the Author

Bradley Hobbs

Every book begins with a story, but every story ultimately points to Jesus Christ. My prayer is that these pages encourage hearts, strengthen faith, and lead readers to the Savior who changes lives.

A Personal Welcome

Thank You for Visiting

Welcome, and thank you for taking the time to visit my website.

My name is Bradley Hobbs, and my greatest desire is to glorify Jesus Christ through every book I write. Whether you have been walking with God for many years or are searching for hope for the very first time, I pray these pages encourage your heart and point you to the One who saves.

Writing has become one of the ways God has allowed me to share His truth, His grace, and His invitation to every person regardless of their past. Every message begins and ends with Jesus Christ.

Why I Write

Every Book Points to Jesus Christ

Writing has never been about simply publishing books. It has always been about sharing the life changing message of Jesus Christ. Every page I write is my prayer that someone will discover the hope, forgiveness, redemption, and assurance that can only be found in Him.

Some readers come with questions. Others carry pain, disappointment, church hurt, fear, or regret. Many simply want to know whether God still sees them. My desire is that every book reminds people that Jesus Christ still calls, still forgives, and still transforms lives today.

If these books accomplish anything, I pray they encourage people to open the Scriptures, seek the Lord with all their hearts, and place their confidence in the Savior rather than in themselves.

My Calling

Serving Through Writing and Ministry

God has blessed me with opportunities to serve through teaching, ministry, and writing. Each has strengthened my conviction that every person matters to God and every life can be transformed through Jesus Christ.

Whether speaking with someone face to face, encouraging a church, teaching children, or writing a book, my purpose remains the same: to glorify Jesus Christ and faithfully share His Word with love, humility, and truth.

I believe the gospel is still the answer for our generation. It reaches beyond labels, backgrounds, failures, and opinions, offering hope to everyone who comes to Christ in faith.
