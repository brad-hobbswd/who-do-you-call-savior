About the Book

Who Do You Call Savior?

Every story is different. Every soul needs the same Savior. This book invites every reader to discover the hope, redemption, forgiveness, and assurance found in Jesus Christ.

"This book is not about deciding whose sins are worse. It is about the One whose blood is greater than every sin."

The Heart Behind the Book

Why This Book Was Written

Thank you for taking the time to visit this website and learn more about Who Do You Call Savior?

Every person has a story. Some stories are filled with joy and faith. Others are marked by brokenness, disappointment, fear, regret, loneliness, or questions that seem to have no answers. No two journeys are exactly alike, yet every heart shares the same need.

This book was written with one purpose in mind: to point every reader to Jesus Christ. It is not a book about comparing lives or deciding whose past is more deserving of grace. It is a reminder that salvation has always been found in one Savior, whose mercy reaches farther than our failures and whose love extends to every person willing to come to Him.

Throughout these pages, you will encounter the hope of the gospel, the beauty of God's grace, and the promise of redemption through Jesus Christ. My prayer is that every chapter encourages you to open the Scriptures, seek the Lord with sincerity, and discover the assurance that belongs to those who place their faith in Him.

Whether you have faithfully walked with Christ for many years or are searching for Him for the very first time, this invitation remains the same.

Come and meet the Savior.

The Purpose of This Book

Every Chapter Points to Jesus Christ

This book is an invitation to look beyond opinions, labels, traditions, and personal experiences to discover the unchanging truth of God's Word. Every chapter has been written with the desire to lift up Jesus Christ and remind readers that He alone is our Savior.

These pages do not seek to elevate one person above another or measure whose sins are greater. Instead, they point to the cross, where every person stands equally in need of God's grace. The invitation of the gospel is extended to all because Christ died for all.

As you continue reading, you will discover biblical truths about salvation, grace, forgiveness, redemption, faith, obedience, and the assurance we have through Jesus Christ. My hope is that this book strengthens believers, encourages those who are searching, and reminds every reader that God's invitation is still open today.

What You'll Discover

A Journey Through the Gospel

Throughout these pages, you will discover the hope of the gospel, the beauty of God's grace, the assurance of salvation, and the invitation that Jesus Christ extends to every person.

Each chapter builds upon the one before it, exploring questions about faith, forgiveness, redemption, obedience, and the confidence we can have in Christ through His finished work on the cross.

Whether you have followed Jesus Christ for many years or are beginning to seek Him for the very first time, this book is an invitation to know Him more deeply through His Word and to experience the transforming power of His grace.
