A Message for Every Heart

Every person has a story. Some have walked through addiction. Others have experienced adultery, theft, violence, fear, shame, loneliness, or deep rejection. Some have been wounded by the world. Others have been wounded by the church. Some identify as LGBTQIA+. Others have struggled with pride, self righteousness, anger, greed, or hidden sin. No matter our background, every one of us has something in common. We all need a Savior.
