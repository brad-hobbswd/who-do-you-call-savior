# Who Do You Call Savior?

**Who Do You Call Savior?** is a Christian book by **Bradley Hobbs** that invites readers to examine the most important question they will ever answer:

> **Who do you call Savior?**

Every story is different. Every soul needs the same Savior.

Through Scripture, biblical teaching, and personal reflection, this book points readers to Jesus Christ and encourages readers to build their faith upon the Word of God.
