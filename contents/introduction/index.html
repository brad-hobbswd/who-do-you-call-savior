Who Do You Call Savior?

Introduction

Every story is different. Every soul needs the same Savior.

Every Story Is Different. Every Soul Needs the Same Savior.

Before you begin this journey, I want to ask you one question.

Who do you call Savior?

It may seem like a simple question, but it is the most important one you will ever answer. Before anyone knows your story, your background, or the mistakes and victories that have shaped your life, God already knows your heart.

Every person has a different story. Some have experienced joy, while others have carried pain, disappointment, fear, addiction, loneliness, or regret. Some wounds are visible, while others are hidden so deeply that no one else knows they exist.

Although our stories are different, we all share the same need.

We all need Jesus Christ.

This book is not about comparing lives or deciding whose past is worse. It is about discovering the One who came to save every person who is willing to come to Him in faith. His mercy reaches the broken, His grace reaches the weary, and His invitation remains open today.

As you read, I encourage you to keep your Bible open. Search the Scriptures for yourself and allow God's Word to speak to your heart. My prayer is not that you remember my words, but that you come to know Jesus more deeply and trust Him more completely.

Thank you for allowing me to walk beside you for a little while. I pray these pages strengthen your faith, encourage your heart, and remind you that no matter where your story began, it can be transformed by the Savior who still changes lives.

Now, let us begin the journey together.

Neither is there salvation in any other: for there is none other name under heaven given among men, whereby we must be saved.
Acts 4:12

The Journey Begins Here

Now, let us begin.

Turn the page with an open Bible, an open heart, and a willingness to discover the Savior who still changes lives.
